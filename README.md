UK Enterprise Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard

Project Overview

This project is a business intelligence dashboard built in Microsoft Power BI to analyze enterprise sales performance across multiple regions, products, customers, and sales representatives.

The dashboard transforms raw sales records into actionable business intelligence by tracking revenue generation, profitability, customer performance, and regional sales trends.

⸻

Business Problem

Organizations generate large volumes of sales data daily but often struggle to identify:

* Which regions generate the highest revenue
* Which products contribute most to profitability
* Which customers generate the highest business value
* Whether sales performance is improving or declining over time
* Areas requiring management intervention

This dashboard was developed to solve these challenges through interactive visual analytics.

Business Impact

Data Pipeline Architecture

The dashboard was powered by a structured SQL database containing sales transactions, customer records, regional performance metrics, and profitability data.

Before visualization, SQL queries were used to:

* Extract relevant business records
* Aggregate revenue and profit metrics
* Organize customer performance data
* Prepare regional sales summaries
* Maintain data consistency for reporting

The processed dataset was then connected to Power BI where data modelling, KPI creation, DAX calculations, and interactive dashboard development were completed.
